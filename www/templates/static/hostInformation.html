M-Lab is a consortium of research, industry, and public interest partners dedicated to providing an ecosystem for the open, verifiable measurement of global network performance. As a collaborative effort, M-Lab depends on the support and involvement of likeminded people and organizations.

Please submit this application if you are interested in hosting servers for an M-Lab site. Groups hosting an M-Lab site will also find the document how to deploy a new M-Lab site useful.
